Principal's Message

Welcome to Kashiram Memorial College, Manpur Baheri (Bareilly). Our mission is to provide quality education that blends tradition with modern learning. We believe in nurturing students with values, discipline, and excellence.

College History & Mission

Kashiram Memorial College was established to serve the local community with affordable and quality higher education. Our vision is to empower students with knowledge and skills that prepare them for future challenges.
