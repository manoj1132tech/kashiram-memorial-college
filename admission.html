Eligibility Criteria

  • 12th Pass from any recognized Board.
  • Minimum 45% marks for Gen/OBC candidates.
  • Minimum 40% marks for SC/ST candidates.

Required Documents

  • High School & Intermediate Marksheets
  • Transfer Certificate (TC) & CC
  • Aadhar Card Photocopy
  • Passport size photos (6 nos.)
  • Caste & Income Certificate
